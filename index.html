Main Inhabitants

Guppies Plecos Honey Gourami
Guppies are a schooling fish with many beautiful color varieties! They are a live birthing species and you'll be sure to see babies in your tank if you keep both males and females together. Bristlenose plecos are a hardier fish that can handle varied PH and mild ammonia spikes. Great for beginners! Honey gourami have the ability to both breathe underwater and above the water. You might notice them come to the surface of the water and take gulps of air!
